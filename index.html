The Infinite Build is an interdimensional hacker group.

For now, you can think of it like an online hackathon, but without judges, sponsors, deadlines, or pitches.

And the prize is... more hacking.

Actually, the prize is the support we give each other.

How It Works

Building can get isolating. So every week, we meet up to screenshare our live dev environments. We share what we're stuck on or what we're having fun with or what we figured out. It's a fun way to learn new stuff, and keep momentum going.

And chat's always there to ask for help (or just a friendly ear) when you need it.

What's with the dolphins?

The dolphins knew the planet was ending and left, grinning. So long and thanks for all the fish! We swim around VC funnels, insider grant access, and sponsored hackathons. We see the problems with those games. We play differently.

Building our own environment

We're also building the tools for Infinite Build together, to make it fork-friendly and anti-capture from the core.

We're working on cryptographic systems that give each builder control of their own experience. Unix-like, composable tools that let unique peer-support spaces emerge and morph around each builder as needed.
