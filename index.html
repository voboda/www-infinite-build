How It Works

Building can get isolating. So every week, we meet up to screenshare our live dev environments. We share what we're stuck on or what we're having fun with or what we figured out. It's a fun way to learn new stuff, and keep momentum going.

And chat's always there to ask for help (or just a friendly ear) when you need it.

What's with the dolphins?

Humans think they're the most intelligent because they make things like the wheel, New York, wars, and venture capital. Dolphins just play in the water. They think they're smarter for exactly that reason.

We're in the system. We see the extraction. But we play differently.

Building our own environment

We're also building the tools for The Infinite Build together, to make it anti-capture down to its fibres.

We're working on cryptographic systems that give each builder control of their own experience. Unix-like, composable, mostly harmless. A toolbelt that let's you conjure peer-support spaces around your needs.

Come play

No application. No evaluators. Just answer a few questions from the heart to open yourself to help.

You'll sign and submit them using familiar tools in a new way. (An invitation to recognising new possibilities.) Your answers get shared with others joining at the same time, so we all know how we can help each other.
