Infinite Build

Infinite Build is a decentralised hackathon in perpetual motion—a space for constant exploration, building, and collaboration. No competition. No pitching. No pressure. Just creativity and peer support.

With a focus on public goods, hyperstructures, open-source tools, composable games, and FOSS, Infinite Build is for creators who think there's more to this than extracting value.

The Continuum

A continuous program for building. Monthly weekend jams help with friendly bursts of creativity, while the time in between is for quiet progress and exploring. Build at your own pace with a supportive network.

The Caravan

A peer-driven funding system for builders, by builders. Trade a slice of your project’s upside for a portfolio of projects doing the same: gain upiside exposure, mentorship, and deeper support. Move from grants to sustainability.
